Description

The Avengers are a fictional superhero team from Marvel Comics who first appeared in The Avengers #1 in September 1963. Stan Lee and Jack Kirby created the team, who are known as "Earth's Mightiest Heroes" and are dedicated to protecting the world from various threats. The Avengers' lineup has changed frequently over time, and has included some of the most popular characters in the Marvel Comics universe. The original Avengers included Iron Man,Ant-Man, Hulk, Thor, and the Wasp, and Captain America later joined after being revived from ice.
