Avengers: Infinity War is a 2018 Marvel Cinematic Universe (MCU) movie about the Avengers and their allies trying to stop Thanos from destroying the universe by collecting the Infinity Stones. The movie is 2 hours and 29 minutes long and has a PG-13 rating. It includes sci-fi action, extreme violence, and some language. In the movie, Thanos, an intergalactic despot, seeks the six Infinity Stones, which are said to be the most powerful artifacts in the universe. He plans to use them in an Infinity Gauntlet to make anything he wants happen with a snap of his fingers
